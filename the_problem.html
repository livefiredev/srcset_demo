The Problem

If we target a 1920px width desktop, the image on the right will take up max: 1920/2 = 960px.

Since this page is responsive, we are also targeting a 1170px iPhone13. So, we need to load a 1170px image. Because on the phone, the image will be going end to end.

So, since we are trying to target both the devices at once, the image is much larger than needed for desktop.
